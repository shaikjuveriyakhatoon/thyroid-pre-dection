Yoga for Thyroid Health

  • Sarvangasana (Shoulder Stand): Stimulates the thyroid gland.
  • Halasana (Plow Pose): Enhances circulation to the neck area.
  • Matsyasana (Fish Pose): Opens the throat and promotes thyroid health.
  • Setu Bandhasana (Bridge Pose): Gently stretches the neck and throat.
  • Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose): Activates and strengthens the neck area.

Meditation Practices

For Pregnant Women

  • Mindfulness Meditation: Reduces stress and promotes hormonal balance.
  • Guided Visualization: Focus on positive energy and relaxation.

For Non-Pregnant Women

  • Chakra Meditation: Focus on the throat chakra (Vishuddha) to improve thyroid function.
  • Breath Awareness: Deep breathing techniques like Anulom Vilom.
